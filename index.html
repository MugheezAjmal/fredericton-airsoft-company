The Field

New Brunswick's
Largest Airsoft Field

At ~60 acres, our field is the largest in the province — divided into four areas: West Wood, East Wood, South Wood, and a central Bog.

West Wood and East Wood are the most developed, with 13 bases and ~3km of marked trails between them. Every base, trail, and intersection has its own Location and Directional signs — you'll never be completely lost in the woods.

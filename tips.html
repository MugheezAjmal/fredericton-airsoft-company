Gear Guide

What to Wear

Category 01

Clothing

Best: Any camouflage pattern
Good: Dark naturals — black, brown, or green
Minimum: Long pants + long-sleeved shirt
Shorts are discouraged
Hats, head wraps & gloves are encouraged
Avoid orange, yellow or red — reserved for Game Masters & hit indicators

FAC does not provide clothing, coveralls, or tactical vests/gear with rental kits.

Category 02

Footwear

Recommended: Hiking boots
Recommended: Combat or rubber boots
Any footwear with good ankle support
Running shoes & cleats allowed if no boots
Strictly Prohibited
NO SANDALS

The field is a woodlot. Protect your feet.

The field is uneven woodland terrain — ankle support is critical for safety.

Category 03

Eye Wear

Rental masks are provided with every kit
Wear contacts whenever possible if you have prescription lenses
Rental masks fit most small-medium prescription frames
Bring anti-fog spray for prescription lenses

Eye protection is mandatory at all times on the field. The JT Proflex X mask is included in all rental kits.

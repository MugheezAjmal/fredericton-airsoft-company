What You Get

Everything Included

Private events include full access to the field, game master supervision, and all the essentials.

Airsoft & Nerf Available

Choose your game type or mix both. Nerf events are great for younger players or first-timers. Airsoft for experienced crews.

Birthdays & Parties

The perfect setting for a memorable birthday. Bring your crew, we'll handle the game. Great for ages 10 and up.

Grill & Picnic Tables

On-site grill and picnic tables for your group. Bring your own food and drinks and make a full day of it.

Bottled Water Included

Bottled water is provided at every private event. The field is a woodlot with no running water — we've got you covered.

Game Master Supervision

A FAC Game Master will run and referee your event. Safety and fun are always the priority.

60 Acres — Exclusive Access

Your group gets exclusive access to the entire 60-acre field — no shared games, no strangers. Just your crew.

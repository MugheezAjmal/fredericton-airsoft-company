Overview

60 Acres of Fun

At ~60 acres, FAC Field is the largest airsoft field in New Brunswick — divided into four distinct zones that can be played individually or combined for large-scale events.

West Wood and East Wood are the most developed areas, connected by ~3km of marked trails with Location Signs at every base and Directional Signs at every intersection. You will never be completely lost in the woods.

Bases are built from a mix of pallet structures, brush piles, and tarp walls. Each base is set up to work as a stand-alone field for small games, or combined with others for large events.

The FAC Home Field does not have a civic address. It is a woodlot on Banks Road — a dirt road with no street sign. Navigate to the reference address above, then look for the FAC sign in a tree at Banks Road's entrance.
